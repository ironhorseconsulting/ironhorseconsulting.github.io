Who We Are

Iron Horse Consulting is a student-led business consulting firm that seeks to help small businesses in the San Francisco East Bay achieve growth and reach their goals. By connecting traditional consulting techniques with the new innovation of today's youth, we believe we are best equipped to support small businesses in their quest for growth and impact. Money isn't our number one priority, hence why all of our services are free. What matters most to us is the success of our clients and the positive impact they bring to our communities, and this is what we hope to achieve.

Partners and Staff

Established in the spring of 2024, Iron Horse Consulting was founded by business-driven and
highly motivated high school students at California High School in San Ramon. They seek to expand into other communities and join forces
with other high schoolers to increase Iron Horse Consulting's impact on the community.
